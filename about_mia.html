About Young Voices

Young Voices was created by a group of young Teenage girls who see a big issue with Chicago's crime, gang violence, poverty and homelessness. So we created our website to let people have a voice, and to express their feels about the violence and if they had some personal experience with the violence or how it affects their everyday lifestyle.

We hope that this website influences people to want to put their story out there openly and anonymously.

We've worked really hard for this and we only had a week to try and make a difference so we hope people enjoy it!

Please get to know about us individually below!!! If you have any questions please email us at: braveyoungvoices6@gmail.com
